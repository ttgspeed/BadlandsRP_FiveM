Important Information

MICROPHONE is required to play on the server. You are expected to put effort forward and RP at all times. You may use /ooc in chat if you need to break character to ask a question or provide help during an RP situation.


Getting Started

  • You can change your name, and age at City Hall
  • Pressing M will bring up your interaction/player/car menu (arrows to navigate, enter to select, backspace to close current menu)
  • Pressing L will toggle the door locks on cars you own
  • Pressing B will toggle your ability to point
  • Pressing ~ will toggle your voice volume
  • Pressing X will put your hands up/down
  • /ooc for out of character chat
  • Don't forget to eat and drink
