Important Information

The server is still in its public testing phase. It is possible there may be medium to large scale changes to create the most balanced experience. Sign up on the forums to keep up to date with changes and to provide feedback.


Getting Started

  • You can change your name, age, gender at City Hall
  • Pressing M will bring up your interaction/player/car menu (arrows to navigate, enter to select, backspace to close current menu)
  • Pressing L will toggle the door locks on cars you own
  • Pressing ~ will toggle your voice volume
  • Pressing X will put your hands up/down
  • /ooc for out of character chat
  • Don't forget to eat and drink
